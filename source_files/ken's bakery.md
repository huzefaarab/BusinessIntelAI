# Ken's Bakery

Ken's Bakery is a premium artisanal bakery located in Pune, Maharashtra.

The bakery specializes in:
- Sourdough breads
- Croissants
- Cheesecakes
- Custom celebration cakes
- Cookies and pastries

The business primarily targets:
- College students
- Working professionals
- Families living nearby
- Customers looking for premium baked products.

Ken's Bakery has been operating since 2020 and has over 500 positive customer reviews across various online platforms.

Their best-selling products include:
- Chocolate Croissants
- Red Velvet Cheesecake
- Garlic Sourdough Bread
- Custom Birthday Cakes

The bakery actively posts on Instagram and occasionally collaborates with local cafes.

Customers frequently appreciate:
- Fresh ingredients
- Fast delivery
- Premium packaging
- Friendly customer service

The business currently offers:
- Same day delivery within Pune.
- Custom cake orders.
- Corporate gifting packages.
- Weekend bakery workshops.

Additional Information:
- Located near a college campus.

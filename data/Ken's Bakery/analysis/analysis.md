**Client:** Ken’s Bakery, Pune, Maharashtra

### 1. What the Business Sells

*   **Observed Facts:** 
    *   Ken’s Bakery is a premium artisanal bakery specializing in:
        *   **Breads:** Sourdough breads (specifically Garlic Sourdough Bread).
        *   **Viennoiserie & Pastry:** Croissants (specifically Chocolate Croissants), cookies, and pastries.
        *   **Cakes & Desserts:** Cheesecakes (specifically Red Velvet Cheesecake) and custom celebration/birthday cakes.
*   **Assumptions:** 
    *   Given their collaborations with local cafes, they may also sell or supply beverage-compatible items or wholesale baked goods, though this is not explicitly detailed.
*   **Missing Information:** 
    *   The complete product catalog and pricing structure.
    *   Whether they offer gluten-free, vegan, sugar-free, or eggless options.
    *   Whether they sell beverages (tea, coffee, etc.) alongside their baked goods.

### 2. Target Audience

*   **Observed Facts:** 
    *   College students (due to proximity to a college campus).
    *   Working professionals.
    *   Families living nearby the bakery.
    *   Customers looking specifically for premium baked products.
*   **Assumptions:** 
    *   **College Students:** Likely driven by affordable indulgence, aesthetic/Instagrammable products, and study-friendly snacks.
    *   **Working Professionals & Nearby Families:** Likely driven by convenience, high-quality ingredients, weekend treats, and milestone celebrations (custom cakes).
    *   **Corporate Clients:** Targeted via their corporate gifting packages.
*   **Missing Information:** 
    *   The exact demographic split (e.g., which of these segments contributes most to revenue).
    *   Psychographic details (e.g., spending power of the local college students versus the families nearby).

### 3. Strongest Offers

*   **Observed Facts:** 
    *   **Best-Selling Products:** Chocolate Croissants, Red Velvet Cheesecake, Garlic Sourdough Bread, and Custom Birthday Cakes.
    *   **Service & Experiential Offers:** Same-day delivery within Pune, custom cake orders, corporate gifting packages, and weekend bakery workshops.
*   **Assumptions:** 
    *   The weekend workshops act as a powerful brand-building and community-engagement tool rather than just a revenue stream.
    *   Same-day delivery is a major competitive advantage for last-minute celebratory needs or spontaneous cravings in Pune.
*   **Missing Information:**

### 5. Customer Needs

*   **Observed Facts:** 
    *   High-quality, fresh ingredients.
    *   Speed and convenience (fast delivery/same-day delivery).

*   **Observed Facts:**
